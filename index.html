Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does the word counter work?

TextTally uses JavaScript to analyze the text you input in real-time. It splits your text by whitespace to determine the word count and uses punctuation marks to identify sentences. Everything happens instantly as you type.

Is TextTally free to use?

Yes! TextTally is a completely free online word counter. We believe in providing high-quality utility tools without any barriers like paywalls or mandatory registrations.

Does TextTally save my text?

Absolutely not. Your text is processed entirely within your own browser. We do not have a database for your text, and nothing is ever sent to our servers. Your privacy is guaranteed.

What is reading time?

Reading time is an estimate of how long it will take for a person to read your text, based on an average speed of 200 words per minute. This is a standard metric used by many publishing platforms.
