About TextTally

The clean, minimal, blazing-fast word and character counter.

Our Philosophy

TextTally was founded with a single mission: to create the cleanest, fastest, and most focused text statistics tool on the web. We believe that professional utilities shouldn't be cluttered with intrusive display advertisements, heavy analytics scripts, or pop-up boxes requesting email registrations.

Whether you are a student editing an essay to meet strict guidelines, a novelist counting chapters, a marketer crafting high-converting copy, or a publisher formatting summaries, TextTally gives you instant feedback precisely when you need it.

🔒 Privacy-First Guarantee

Your writing is personal, confidential, and valuable. We respect that absolutely. That is why we designed TextTally to be an offline-first, client-side utility:

  • Zero Server Uploads: Every calculation, text count, paragraph analysis, and grammar check is performed entirely in your web browser. Your text is never sent to our servers.
  • Local Persistence: Using the manual Save button stores your text inside your own device's Secure LocalStorage. Nobody else has access to it.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your typing habits, keep copy logs, or construct user profile lists.

Core Features

  • Blazing-Fast Calculations: Instantly displays counts for words, characters, sentences, paragraphs, and read/speak durations.
  • Responsive Formatting: Adapts fluidly across smart mobile displays, tablets, laptops, and wide screen monitors.
  • Interactive Grammar Engine: Scans syntax and spelling directly in your browser using secure client models and rules.
  • Visual Settings: Customize typography styles and theme backgrounds like Sepia, Forest Green, or Deep Ocean to relieve eye strain.

🔒 Privacy First: Your text never leaves your device. All calculations are performed instantly in your browser.
